# Company Policy Handbook

## Refunds

Our refund policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase.
Items must be unused and in original packaging. Digital goods are non-refundable
unless required by law.

## Shipping

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days.
Express shipping arrives in 2 business days within the continental US.
International shipping can take 10-20 business days depending on customs.

## Warranty

Products include a 12-month limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.
The warranty does not cover accidental damage, misuse, or normal wear.
